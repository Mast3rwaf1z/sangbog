What shall we do with the drunken \st{sailor} BoAnd,
what shall we do with the drunken \st{sailor} BoAnd,
what shall we do with the drunken \st{sailor} BoAnd,
earl-aye in the morning?
\emph{Hooray and up she rises,
      hooray and up she rises,
      hooray and up she rises,
      earl-ay in the morning.}
\end{vers}
